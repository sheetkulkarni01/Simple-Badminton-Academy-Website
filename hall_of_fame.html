HALL OF FAME





Sheetal Kulkarni of our academy has won the V.T.U Belagavi Zone Shuttle Badminton tournament held on 4th September 2017 in V.T.U Belagavi. Sheetal defeated B.V.B college, Hubli 21-15,21-14 in the finals after knocking out K.L.E College of Engineering,Belagavi in the semi-finals earlier in the day.














Sheetal Kulkarni of our academy has won the National level Fest " ZEST'17 " in Shuttle Badminton category held on 28th January 2017 in College of Engineering, Pune. Sheetal defeated MKSSS's Cummins College of Engineering, Pune 21-12,19-21,21-11 in the finals after defeating College of Engineering,Pune in the semi-finals earlier in the day.










Shekhar Somnath of our academy has brought laurels to our academy by winning the 22nd Senior National Championship in Under 19 category held on 25th November 2016 in Hyderabad. Shekhar knocked out Ponnappa of Chennai, 21-20, 19-21,21-18 in the finals after defeating Srikanth of Mumbai in the semi-finals earlier in the day.











Imran afreedi and Siddharth Jha of our academy have bagged the runner up medals in the All India Junior Championship held on 12th July 2016 in Agra. Imran knocked out Chirag of Bangalore, 21-20, 21-13 in the finals after defeating Vishal of Assam in the quarters. Siddharth Jha won against Manthan of Delhi in the quarters.
