ABOUT OUR ACADEMY

Lord of Strings Academy was established in 2009 and within a short span of its existence, it has already established itself as one of the top ten Sports Academies in India. In the contemporary times when lifestyle of the people has become sedentary, we at Lord of Strings Academy have something for everyone. For us, complete health for all is equally essential as healthy citizens contribute to bring name, laurels, and glory for the nation. Lord of Strings Academy is pioneer in the field of shuttle badminton when it comes to providing globally approved infrastructure, customized sports curriculum, dietary plans and mentorship from renowned & legendary coaches. Our Academy programmes are well designed to ensure that every trainee gets optimum benefit by undertaking them. Lord of Strings academy is one of the best sports academies in the North Karnataka region, offering the finest equipment and training in outfield sports, and a chance to grow your sporting career.
